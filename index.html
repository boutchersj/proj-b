Bruce Campbell

None other than the Chin himself. He can often be found slaying Deadites or running around Miami with Michael Westen.

Big Chin Wins

  • 1981: Starred in the cult classic, Evil Dead, and did the camera work for the movie
  • 1987: Starred in Evil Dead II
  • 1992: Starred in the 3-quel to Evil Dead called Army of Darkness
  • 2001: Cameo in Spider-Man movie
  • 2004: Cameo in Spider-Man 2 movie AND narrated the Spider-Man 2 video game
  • 2005: Played a minor character in Disney's Sky High, and contributed to other animated movies like Cars 2
  • 2007: Made a cameo in Spider-Man 3 movie
  • 2007-2013: Played the iconic role of Sam Axe in the USA Network's series, Burn Notice
  • 2011: Starred in the Burn Notice spin-off movie, The Fall of Sam Axe
